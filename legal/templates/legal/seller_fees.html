Seller Fees

Local Market NE charges a 10% marketplace fee on each completed sale.

How the Fee Works

  • The 10% fee is automatically deducted from each applicable order line.
  • The remaining seller net amount is paid out through Stripe Connect.
  • There are no hidden platform fee deductions beyond what is disclosed in policy/settings.

Example Calculations

Example 1:
Product price: $20.00
Seller fee (10%): $2.00
Amount seller receives: $18.00
Example 2:
Product price: $100.00
Seller fee (10%): $10.00
Amount seller receives: $90.00

If you have fee questions, contact localmarketne@gmail.com.
