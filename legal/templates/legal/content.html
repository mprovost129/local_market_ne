Content & Safety Policy

All listings and user content must be accurate, lawful, and safe for marketplace use.

Prohibited Content and Listings

  • Illegal products or services
  • Weapons, alcohol, tobacco, or regulated goods not allowed by platform policy
  • Fraudulent, deceptive, or misleading listings
  • Harassing, hateful, abusive, or threatening content
  • Content that infringes intellectual property rights

Seller Responsibility

Sellers must ensure their listings are accurate, lawful, and fulfillable as described.

Enforcement

Local Market NE may remove content, suspend listings, or restrict accounts for policy violations.

Reporting

Users may report suspected violations through platform reporting tools or by email.

Contact

Report content concerns to localmarketne@gmail.com.
