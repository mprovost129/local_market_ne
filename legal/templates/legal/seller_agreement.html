Seller Agreement

This Seller Agreement governs seller participation on Local Market NE.

Seller Obligations

  • Must be 18 years or older to sell on this platform.
  • Provide accurate listings, pricing, and fulfillment details.
  • Deliver products/services as described and communicate with buyers in a timely manner.
  • Comply with marketplace policies, prohibited item rules, and applicable laws.

Stripe and Payouts

Sellers must complete Stripe Connect onboarding to accept platform checkout payments and receive payouts.

Marketplace Sales Fee

Local Market NE charges a 10% marketplace sales fee on each completed sale processed through the platform.

  • The 10% fee is calculated from each order line amount.
  • The fee is deducted before seller net payout is transferred.
  • Tips are treated as seller earnings per platform logic; platform fees remain non-refundable unless required by law.

For examples, see the Seller Fees page.

Payment Integrity and Fee Circumvention

Sellers may not circumvent platform fees by directing or encouraging buyers to complete payment outside the platform for products or services that were initiated, negotiated, engaged, or agreed through Local Market NE.

  • Applicable transactions must be recorded and completed through approved platform checkout flows.
  • Sellers must maintain accurate transaction records and cooperate with reasonable compliance reviews.
  • Confirmed attempts to evade marketplace fees may result in listing removal, payout holds, account suspension, or permanent removal.

Policy Updates

Local Market NE may update seller terms and fee policies. Continued use of seller tools after updates indicates acceptance of revised terms.

Contact

Seller agreement questions can be sent to localmarketne@gmail.com.
