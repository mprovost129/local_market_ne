Privacy Policy

Local Market NE collects only the information needed to operate the marketplace and support transactions.

Information We Collect

How We Use Information

We use information to provide marketplace services, process payments, support users, prevent abuse, and improve platform reliability.

Payments are processed through Stripe. Local Market NE does not store full payment card details.

We share information only as required to run the marketplace (for example, with payment providers and fulfillment-related parties) or when required by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical controls to protect data, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Your Rights
