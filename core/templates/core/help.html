Buying

Find local products and services, checkout securely, and track your orders.
  • Use Products or Services to browse, then filter by category and location.
  • Add items to your cart and start checkout (pickup, delivery, or shipping when available).
  • After purchase, see status updates under My Orders.
  • Only verified purchases can leave reviews.
My Orders FAQs

Selling

List products or services, fulfill orders, and get paid through Stripe.
  • Create listings with clear photos, accurate descriptions, and honest lead times.
  • For payouts, sellers must complete Stripe Connect onboarding.
  • Use your Seller Dashboard to manage listings and fulfill orders.
  • Refund rules depend on the item type and the seller’s policy. Marketplace fees are non-refundable.
