Tips

Practical advice for better listings, smoother fulfillment, and happier customers.
Shop Services FAQs
Photos sell
Bright, clean photos beat perfect wording every time.
  • Use natural light (window light) and a plain background.
  • Show scale (a hand, ruler, or common object).
  • Add at least one close-up of details/condition.
Descriptions that reduce returns
Be specific, especially about condition and what’s included.
  • Call out any flaws clearly (scratches, wear, missing parts).
  • List dimensions, materials, and compatibility where relevant.
  • Set accurate lead times for made-to-order items.
Fulfillment clarity
Buyers love knowing exactly what happens next.
  • For pickup: share a general area and pickup windows (exact address only when ready).
  • For delivery: state your delivery radius and any fees.
  • For services: define what’s included and what counts as an add-on.
Fulfillment Policy
Answer questions fast
Fast replies boost trust and conversions.
  • Use Q&A to clarify details publicly for future buyers.
  • Be polite and specific; avoid “DM me” for everything.
  • If something is unavailable, update your listing quickly.
